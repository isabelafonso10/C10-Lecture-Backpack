Welcome to the Moo Deng Clan!

About Moo Deng

Moo Deng was born on 10 July 2024 to parents Tony and Jonah.
She has two full siblings (Nadet and Moo Tun) and four half-siblings (Ko, Kanya, Phalo, and Moo Wan).
Her name was chosen through a public poll, with over 20,000 people voting for "Moo Deng", translating to "bouncy pork" or "bouncy pig".

Fun Facts

  • On September 28, Moo Deng was the subject of a Saturday Night Live sketch.
  • Moo Deng is noted to be more playful and energetic than other hippopotamuses.

Top Achievements

  1. Moo Deng has become the subject of many pieces of fan art.
  2. Companies have produced merchandise based on Moo Deng, including a cake shop.
